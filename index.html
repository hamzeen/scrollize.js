This plugin was developed for two reasons, to be
simple & responsive.

It doesn't alter or introduce any attribute on your markup except for the background position of the element(s) that you request to do parallax.
Its responsiveness means that it works seamlessly on mobile browsers too which many seem to prefer turning off.
